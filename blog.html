ISSUING OF SEMESTER RESULT SHEETS

Please be informed that issuing of semester result sheets is based on the following conditions w.e.f. 01.04.2018:

1. Semester result sheets are issued for the undergraduates who have completed all semesters  of the degree programmes and who have not been conferred on relevant degrees at the annual convocation.
2. The Undergraduates who have not completed all semesters of the degree programmes are issued with semester result sheets based on the following occasions, if only relevant submitted:
    a. To apply for a scholarship.
    b. To submit as a progress report to another institute/ organization.
    c. Any other acceptable reasons with documentary proof.
